Travel Tips

  • Get a Suica/Pasmo card for transportation
  • Convenience stores (7-Eleven, FamilyMart, Lawson) are everywhere and very useful
  • Most places are cash-only, carry yen
  • Tipping is not customary
  • Learn basic phrases: Arigatou gozaimasu (thank you), Sumimasen (excuse me)

Food to Try

Tokyo

  • Fresh sushi and sashimi
  • Tempura

Kyoto

  • Kaiseki (traditional multi-course meal)
  • Matcha desserts and tea in Uji
  • Yudofu (tofu hot pot)

Hokkaido Specialties

  • Fresh seafood (crab, uni, ikura)
  • Hokkaido ramen (miso-based)
  • Jingisukan (grilled lamb)
  • Soup curry
  • Dairy products (milk, ice cream)
  • LeTAO cheesecake (Otaru)
