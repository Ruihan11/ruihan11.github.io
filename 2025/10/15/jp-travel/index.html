Pre-Trip Checklist

  • evisa
  • Book flights
  • Reserve accommodations
  • Get JR Pass (if traveling between cities)
  • Pocket WiFi or SIM card
  • Travel insurance
  • Currency exchange (JPY)
  • Download useful apps (Google Maps, Google Translate, Hyperdia)

Itinerary

Tokyo

  • Shibuya Crossing
  • Senso-ji Temple (Asakusa)
  • Meiji Shrine
  • TeamLab Borderless
  • Tsukiji Outer Market

Kyoto

  • Fushimi Inari Shrine
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)
  • Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Gion District

Osaka

  • Dotonbori
  • Osaka Castle
  • Kuromon Market

Travel Tips

  • Get a Suica/Pasmo card for transportation
  • Convenience stores (7-Eleven, FamilyMart, Lawson) are everywhere and very useful
  • Most places are cash-only, carry yen
  • Tipping is not customary
  • Learn basic phrases: Arigatou gozaimasu (thank you), Sumimasen (excuse me)

Food to Try

  • Ramen
  • Sushi
  • Takoyaki
  • Okonomiyaki
  • Tempura
  • Matcha desserts
